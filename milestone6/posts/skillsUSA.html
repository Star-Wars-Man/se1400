2nd Place at the SkillsUSA State Electronics Technology Competition!

Hey everyone!

I'm thrilled to share some awesome news — I placed 2nd in the SkillsUSA State Competition for Electronics Technology!

This competition was a serious challenge. It tested everything from circuit building and troubleshooting to electronics theory and problem-solving under pressure. I went up against a lot of skilled competitors, and every part of the event pushed me to stay sharp and focused.

One of the highlights was the hands-on troubleshooting event. We had to find hidden faults in a broken circuit, fix them, and get everything working again — all under a time limit. It was intense but incredibly fun!

When they called my name for 2nd place, it felt amazing. All the hours spent studying, building, burning out a few circuits (oops), and learning paid off. It's an experience I’ll never forget, and it’s motivating me to aim even higher next time!

Thanks so much to my mentors, teachers, friends, and family who supported me along the way. This is just the beginning — there's a lot more to come on my electronics journey!

Stay tuned for more projects, experiments, and maybe even some tips on how I prepared for SkillsUSA!

- Jacob
