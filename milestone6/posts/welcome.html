Welcome to Jacob's Robotics!

Hey there, fellow tinkerers and tech enthusiasts!

Welcome to Jacob's Robotics, the ultimate spot for everything electronics—whether you're just starting out or already knee-deep in wires and solder. I'm Jacob, a high school student with a passion for circuits, coding, and making cool stuff that (hopefully) doesn't catch fire.

Here, you'll find:

  • Beginner-friendly guides - because we all start somewhere!
  • DIY projects - from LED light shows to homemade gadgets.
  • Tech breakdowns - how everyday electronics actually work.
  • Fails & fixes - because learning from mistakes is half the fun.

If you love the smell of burnt transistors (or just want to avoid that fate), stick around! Let's learn, build, and maybe even blow some fuses—safely, of course.

Stay curious, stay creative, and let's break some circuits (on purpose)!

- Jacob
